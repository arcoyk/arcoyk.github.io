ACCESS LIMITED

Physical Superimposition

Physical distance is still big while the Internet shortened our visual or audio distance. We need to go and back from room to room to attend meeting. But, wait, we have head, body and arms as common interfaces to act in the physical world. Then, how can we surrogate or be surrogated to share our body? This project seeks interfaces for smooth, efficient and immersive surrogation.

Pilot movies This system records and plays masters' physical movement. Another user can reproduce it by following it with a shadowing-like interface. It aims to record and play physical movement for the era of the Internet of abilities (IoA). The shadowing is effective for iterative learning without a teacher aside. On the contrary, this system aims to controle remote user's bold motion like the body position. The camera on the user's shoulder captures surrounding information with the relationship of the body and the environment. This system is motived by typical TV games where the player controles character's position with his body and the surrounding environment.
